Background

About Me

I am a researcher, communicator, and community advocate at the intersection of strategic communication, applied AI/ML, and cross-sector operations. My journey began in Dhaka, Bangladesh, where I built a 15-year career spanning capital markets, insurance management, and footwear manufacturing — earning the Women Entrepreneurs Award (2020) for my contributions to business leadership and economic empowerment.

Relocating to the United States, I enrolled in the Master of Strategic Communication program at Gannon University in Erie, PA, while actively contributing to the community through survivor advocacy at SafeNet, Inc. and completing the full PCADV training program in trauma-informed care.

My published research spans healthcare imaging AI, NLP-based misinformation detection, dynamic pricing algorithms, and Industry 4.0 digital twin systems. I bring a rare combination of executive leadership, data-driven analytical skills, and deep human empathy to everything I do.

Beyond academia and research, I am a poet — author of nearly 95 poems in Bangla and 15 in English, published in anthologies and newspapers across the US and Bangladesh. I believe words build bridges that data alone cannot cross.
